Quick Examples:

Good: "toy story", "avengers 2012", "dark knight"

Avoid: "the avengers", "avengers, the (2012)", "The Dark Knight"

Pro Tips:

  • Use lowercase letters for consistency
  • Skip articles ("the", "a", "an") at the beginning
  • Add year for specific movie versions
  • Try partial matches if exact search doesn't work
  • Use key words from the movie title
